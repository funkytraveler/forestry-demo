Based on which data center executes your ADL algos, the number of ADL algos you can run simultaneously is limited as follows:

  • Aurora: at least 100 but possibly as many as 400 depending upon system load
  • Bangkok: 100
  • All other co-location facilities: at least 100 but possibly as many as 200 depending upon system load

These limits do not apply to Algo Servers deployed on TT Reserved instances. If you have any questions or are interested in a dedicated Algo Server deployed on a TT Reserved instance, please contact your TT Customer Success representative.
