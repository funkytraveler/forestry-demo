To edit a synthetic spread:

  1. Click the edit icon next to a spread in the Autospreader widget.

    The Edit Spread: (spread name) screen appears.

  2. Make the changes to the spread as needed and click Save.

    If prompted to confirm unsaved changes, click Save Changes.
