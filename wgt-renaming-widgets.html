--- widget: wgt layout: task-tab title: Renaming Widgets AccordionID: wgt-renaming-widgets ---

When you open a widget or create a widget group, you can rename the widget or widget group with a custom name. To rename a widget:

  1. Right-click in the widget title bar and select Rename widget in the context menu.

  2. Type and enter the custom name in the text field that appears.

    The custom name appears in the title bar.

  3. To remove a custom name, right-click in the title bar, select Rename widget, and delete the custom name by hitting the Return key or clicking away from the title bar.

    Your custom name is then replaced with the original widget name.
