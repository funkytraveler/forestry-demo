To launch a Floating Order Book in MD Trader:

  1. Middle-click a working order cell in MD Trader.

    The Floating Order Book opens seeded with the order details.

  2. In the Floating Order Book, you can change the WrkQty (working quantity) or Price. You can also click the Cxl icon to cancel the order, or click the Algo icon to launch an algo to manage the order.
