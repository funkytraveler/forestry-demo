Disconnect: Sets how TT handles an ADL algo order if your browser disconnects from the platform. Note: This functionality applies to ADL algos only. Parent and child TT algo orders continue working in the market if your browser disconnects from TT.
  • Leave: The ADL parent and child orders remain working in the market after a disconnect.
  • Pause: The parent ADL algo order is paused. The logic in the ADL parent algo determines how its working child orders are handled after a disconnect.
  • Cancel: The parent ADL algo order is canceled. The logic in the ADL parent algo determines how its working child orders are handled after a disconnect.
