Note: Before opening Blocktrader, ensure that the BT column is shown in the RFQ Viewer. To show this column, right-click in the RFQ Viewer and select Settings | Set RFQ Viewer columns from the context menu.

To open Blocktrader from the RFQ Viewer:

  1. Select a row in the viewer and click the button in the BT column.

    The Blocktrader widget opens seeded with the leg(s) and market data for the instrument in the row.

    Optionally, you can also right-click a row in the viewer to open the context menu and click Open | Blocktrader.

  2. Configure the block trade in the Blocktrader widget as needed and submit it to the exchange.
