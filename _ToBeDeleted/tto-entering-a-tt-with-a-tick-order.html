To enter a TT With A Tick order:

  1. In the the MD Trader® or Order Ticket widget, choose the contract to trade.
  2. Enter the desired order quantity and price for the order.
  3. Select TT With A Tick for the order type.
  4. Enter the desired With a tick details parameters.
  5. Click OK to close the parameters dialog.
  6. Specify the TIF for the exchange-native child orders submitted by the TT With A Tick order.
  7. Click Buy or Sell to submit the order.
