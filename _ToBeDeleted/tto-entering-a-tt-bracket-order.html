To enter a TT Bracket order in MD Trader®:

  1. Open MD Trader®.
  2. Select TT Bracket for the order type.
  3. Enter the TT Bracket parameters.
  4. Select an account or use the default account.
  5. Specify the TIF for the exchange-native child orders submitted by the order.
  6. Enter the order quantity.
  7. Click a price level for the parent TT Bracket entry order.

To enter a TT Bracket order in the Order Ticket:

  1. Open an Order Ticket.
  2. Select TT Bracket for the order type.
  3. Enter the TT Bracket parameters.
  4. Enter the quantity and price for the entry order.
  5. Select an account or use the default account.
  6. Specify the TIF for the exchange-native child orders submitted by the order.
  7. Click Buy or Sell to submit the order.
