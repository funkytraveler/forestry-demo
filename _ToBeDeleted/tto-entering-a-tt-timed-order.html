To enter a TT Timed order:

  1. In the the MD Trader® or Order Ticket widget, choose the contract to trade.
  2. Enter the desired order quantity and price for the order (price is not required if you want to specify a relative price.)
  3. Select TT Timed for the order type.
  4. Enter the desired Timed order details parameters, and click OK.
  5. Specify the TIF for the exchange-native child orders submitted by the TT Timed order.
  6. Click Buy or Sell to submit the order.
