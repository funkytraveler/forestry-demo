To configure two factor authentication:

  1. Click your account username in the title bar to open the Account Settings page.

  2. On the Account Settings page, click the Security tab and click Edit.

  3. Select a verification method. If you select SMS, enter a Primary Mobile Number. TT will send a confirmation text message to the mobile number that you enter.

  4. Click Save.
