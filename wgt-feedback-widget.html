You can report an incident or send feedback from within the TT application by using the Feedback widget in your workspace. Feedback can be sent for an individual widget or for your entire workspace.

To open the Feedback widget:

  • Right-click in the top title bar of any Trade application widget and select the Contact support... option from the context menu. You can also use the Contact support... option to open the widget and send feedback on one or more selected Fills records in a Fills widget.
  • Click ? Support on the upper right corner of the workspace menu bar and click Send Feedback from the TT Support menu.

Feedback Widget display

The Feedback Widget display consists of the following:

  1. Message area — Type and enter a description of your issue, or provide a question or other feedback.
  2. Include screenshot and data setting — When checked (enabled), this option allows you to include a screenshot of the widget and corresponding user data (account, username, environment, etc.) in the message you send to TT. When unchecked, no screenshot and meta data are sent with the message.
  3. Tabs — The Screenshot tab shows a screen capture of the widget or your entire workspace. The Data tab includes meta data, such as your account, username, environment, etc.
  4. Editing options — Displays Feedback widget icons that allow you to capture an image of your workspace, or edit portions of your screenshot image(s).
  5. Buttons — Used for submitting the feedback to TT or canceling and closing the widget without sending feedback.
