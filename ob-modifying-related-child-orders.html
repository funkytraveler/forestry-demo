Related child orders can be modified using the Order Book. The owner can modify the following for working or partially filled child orders:

  • Quantity — Changes to the child order quantity are allowed as long the total quantity of the parent staged order is not exceeded.
  • Price — Allowed if the price change betters the trade (i.e., buy at a lower price or sell at a higher price).
  • TIF — Changes can be made using the natively supported TIF restrictions.
