--- widget: sm layout: desc-tab title: Order entry for exchange-listed spreads AccordionID: sm-order-entry-for-exchange-listed-spreads ---

Using the Spread Matrix and a floating order entry screen, you can quickly enter orders for all outrights and exchange-listed calendar, Butterfly and Condor spreads within a single window for active spread trading.

When you click a market data cell for an instrument that does not intersect with another instrument in the matrix, a floating order entry widget opens pre-populated with the exchange's price for that instrument. When you click a market data cell where the products intersect in the matrix, a floating order entry widget opens pre-populated with the exchange's price for the calendar spread.

Note: The cells containing spread contract names are highlighted to indicate which contracts you are spread trading.
