If you want to increasing the chances of fully filling an order, you can try to fill the order at a limit price. Hedge Manager will reprice the order to a price level with sufficient liquidity to cover the remaining order quantity. Note that choosing this option might reprice the order multiple ticks across the inside market.

To use Fill with Limit, do either of the following:

  • Click Fill w/ Lmt in the row of the desired order.

  • Select one or more working orders and click Fill w/ Limit in the toolbar.

    Note: You can also click the Fill w/ Limit toolbar button drop-down and click Fill Buys, Fill Sells or Fill All.
