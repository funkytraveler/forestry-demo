Centering the Market

When launching an MD Trader widget or adding a new instrument, the prices are centered around the mid-point of the bid/ask spread. That mid-point is indicated with a bold horizontal line. As bid and ask quantities change, the price column remains static unless the prices are recentered.

You can recenter the prices in all MD Traders in your workspace by taking one of the following actions:

  • Right-click on the Price column of any MD Trader widget.
  • Center-click on the Price column of any MD Trader widget.
  • Press the spacebar on your keyboard with focus on any MD Trader widget.

You can also configure MD Trader to automatically recenter based on a specified number of seconds in the Settings. This automatic recentering only causes the specific MD Trader to recenter.

Any time an MD Trader is recentered, the bold horizontal line that indicates the mid-point of the bid/ask spread is reset.

Reordering columns in MD Trader

You can move the following MD Trader columns: Work, Bids, Price, Asks, and LTQ. Click and drag a column to move it to a different location. For example, you can place the Last-Traded-Quantity (LTQ) and Working Orders (Work) columns side-by-side.
