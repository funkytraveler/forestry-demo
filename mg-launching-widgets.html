The following styles of widgets that can be launched from the Market Grid:

  • Independent widget
  • Linked widget

Launching an independent widget

To open an independent widget pre-populated with the selected instrument:

  1. Right-click on an instrument row and highlight Open.

  2. Select the desired widget and move it to the desired location.

    The new widget opens with no colored border.

Launching a linked widget

From a Market Grid, you can open widgets linked to the selected instrument. The content of the linked widget updates automatically when you select another instrument in the grid.

To launch a linked widget:

  1. Right-click on an instrument row and highlight Launch linked widget.

  2. Select the desired widget and move it to the desired location.

    The new widget opens with a yellow border.

  3. In the Market Grid, select a different instrument.

    Notice the widget now contains the market data for the selected instrument.
