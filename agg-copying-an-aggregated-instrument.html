To copy an aggregated instrument:

  1. In the Aggregator Manager, type an instrument name or keywords in the Search field to find an instrument or to filter the list of available instruments.
  2. Click the checkbox next to an aggregated instrument to select it
  3. Click the Copy button at the top of the widget.

    The copied instrument appears in the list of aggregated instruments.
