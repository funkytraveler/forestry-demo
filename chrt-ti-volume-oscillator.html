The Volume Oscillator displays the difference between two moving averages of a security's volume.

{% include chrt-ti-images.html %}
  • Short Cycle: Number of periods in the fast volume moving average.
  • Long Cycle: Number of periods in the slow volume moving average.
  • Points or Percent: Whether to display the curve as the actual difference or as a percentage of the fast volume moving average.

Formula

\[ VO = \frac{Shorter Moving Average - Longer Moving Average}{Shorter Moving Average} \times 100\]
