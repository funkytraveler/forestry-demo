Elder Ray (ER)

Description

The Elder Ray, developed by Alexander Elder, is comprise of two parts; Bull Power and Bear Power. Bull Power measures whether the high is above or below an EMA. Bear Power measures whether the low is above or below an EMA.

Formula

\[Bull\;Power = High - EMA_{n-period}\]

\[Bear\;Power = Low - EMA_{n-period}\]
