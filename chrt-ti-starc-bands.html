The STARC (Stoller Average Range Channels) Bands indicator calculates upper and lower bands around a simple moving average to predict price movement. A price move toward the upper band may mean a pull back towards the moving average, and a move to the lower band may mean a move up towards the moving average.

{% include chrt-ti-images.html %}
  • {% include ti-period-blurb.html %}
  • MA Period: Number of periods to use for calculating the moving average.
  • Multiplier: Moving average multipler to define the distance to draw the bands away from the ATR.
  • Channel Fill: Whether to shade the region between the upper and lower bands.

Formula

\[ Upper\;STARC\;band = SMA + (ATR * multiplier) \]

\[ Lower\;STARC\;band = SMA - (ATR * multiplier) \]
