TTâ€™s Electronic Eye display consists of the following:

  1. Instrument columns — Name and type of the options instrument
  2. Strike price column — Strike price for the instrument
  3. Expiration columns — Expiry information for the instrument
  4. Underlying price column — Price of the underlying instrument associated with the options instrument
  5. Bid-related columns — Market data relating to bids, including the bid quantity and bid price
  6. Sell Edge column — Sell edge at the bid price
  7. Theoretical values column — Theoretical value of the instrument
  8. ATM Vol column — At-the-money volatility
  9. Ask-related columns — Market data relating to asks, including the ask quantity and ask price
  10. Buy Edge column — Buy edge at the ask price
  11. Volatility and delta columns — Volatilities and deltas for the user-defined, implied, and settlement volatility curves.
  12. Highlighted matches — Options instruments that no longer meet the filter requirements and will be removed in a few seconds, as indicated by the orange background.
  13. Tabs — Tabs allowing you to monitor multiple instruments in a single Electronic Eye
