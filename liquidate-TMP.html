Liquidate button in MD Trader

MD Trader allows you to liquidate your current position quickly when the need arises by automatically submitting orders to offset your position.

When you enable the Show Liquidate button in the global or local settings, MD Trader adds the Liquidate button to MD Trader, as shown.

Note: The Liquidate button is not added for synthetic instruments, such as Autospreader or Aggregator instruments.

When you click Liquidate, MD Trader:

  • Determines the accounts and respective quantities that comprise the position, based on the state of the Filter check box.

  • Cancels all working orders for the instrument submitted by the filtered accounts.

  • For each cancel acknowledgment (ACK) it receives, MD Trader submits an order to close the position, using order types in the following order:

    • Market order if the exchange supports market orders
    • Market Limit Market (MLM) order if supported by the exchange
    • Limit order five ticks into the market, using the following waterfall to determine the "market" price:
      • Bid or Ask price for long and short positions, respectively
      • Last traded price (LTP)
      • Settle price

    • For example, suppose you are not filtering your accounts and you have a short position of 25 with 10 for account A1 and 15 for account A2. To liquidate your position, MD Trader will submit a 10-lot offsetting order for account A1 and a 15-lot offsetting order for account A2.
