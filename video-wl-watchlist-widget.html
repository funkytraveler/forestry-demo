TT’s Watchlist widget provides you the ability monitor exchange-listed instruments, spreads and strategies in a concise display as well as the ability to monitor theoretical values and Greeks for user defined strategies privately before submitting them to an exchange for publication. In this video, we’ll demonstrate how to populate our Watchlist widget using our RFQ Viewer, Options Trade Monitor and Strategy Creation widgets and then use the Watchlist widget to submit RFQs and enter orders.
