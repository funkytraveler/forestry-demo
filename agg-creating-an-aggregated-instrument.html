To create an aggregated instrument:

  1. Click +Create in the Aggregator Manager to open the Create Aggregated Instrument screen.
  2. Configure the Aggregator routing parameters and settings.
  3. View the synthetic market for the aggregated instrument in the preview ladder.

  4. Click Save.

After creating and launching an aggregated instrument, it is available to trade as a product in the Market Explorer. Use the Search box or click Explore in the title bar to find aggregated instruments.
