You can trade from the Electronic Eye by opening a Floating Order Entry widget for the option you want to trade. You can also open linked or unlinked MD Trader and Order Tickets for an options instrument.

Launching a Floating Order Entry widget

You can launch a Floating Order Entry widget by clicking the following column values:

  • Bid or Ask column to submit an order at the current price
  • BidQty or AskQty column to submit an order for the available quantity
  • Buy Edge column to submit an order at the ask price or Sell Edge column to submit an order at the bid price.
  • TV column to submit an order at the theoretical price.

Submitting an order at the current price

To submit an order at the current price:

  1. For the desired contract, click a price in either the Bid or Ask column to display the Floating Order Entry widget.

    The price field is automatically populated with the price you clicked. You can change the price, if desired.

  2. Enter the remaining order information and submit the order.

Submitting an order for the available quantity

To submit an order for the quantity available at a price level:

  1. For the desired contract, click a price in either the BidQty or AskQty column to display the Floating Order Entry widget.

    The quantity field is automatically populated with the quantity you clicked. You can change the quantity, if desired.

  2. Enter the remaining order information and submit the order.

Submitting an order to take advantage of an opportunity

To submit an order at a buy or sell edge to take advantage of a potential opporunity:

  1. For the desired contract, click a price in either the Buy Edge or Sell Edge column to display the Floating Order Entry widget.

    The price field is automatically populated with opposite Ask or Bid price.

  2. Enter the remaining order information and submit the order.

Submitting an order at the theoretical value price

To submit an order at the theoretical value price:

  1. For the desired contract, click the price in the TV column to display the Floating Order Entry widget.

    The price field is automatically populated with the price you clicked. You can change the price, if desired.

  2. Enter the remaining order information and submit the order.
